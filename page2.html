My Favorite Movies

On this page, you will find my top five movies of all time.

These movies range from serious ones to old cartoon movies I would watch as a child.Enjoy!

The Truman Show

Description:

Truman Burbank lives a seemingly perfect life, unaware that his entire world is a massive reality TV show.

As small inconsistencies build up, he starts questioning his reality and pushes against the limits of his carefully controlled world.

This film is both unsettling and inspiring, making you wonder about the nature of free will and how much of our lives are truly our own.

12 angry men

Description:

In a hot, cramped jury room, twelve men debate the fate of a young man accused of murder.

While most are ready to convict without much thought, one juror insists on reviewing the evidence, exposing deep biases and moral struggles along the way.

The film is gripping despite its single setting, proving that dialogue alone can create intense drama and challenge our perceptions of justice.

Howl's Moving Castle

Description:

When Sophie is cursed by a jealous witch and turned into an old woman, she finds refuge in the magical moving castle of the mysterious wizard Howl.

What starts as a journey to break a spell becomes a heartwarming exploration of love, identity, and self-worth.

This film feels like stepping into a dream—visually stunning, whimsical, and deeply emotional in unexpected ways.

Mr. and Mrs. Smith(2005)

Description

A seemingly normal married couple hides a thrilling secret: they’re both highly skilled assassins working for opposing agencies.

When their missions collide, their marriage turns into a chaotic battleground of deception, action, and romance.

It’s stylish, fun, and filled with chemistry—reminding us that sometimes, relationships thrive on a little danger.

Ocean's Eight

Description:

Debbie Ocean gathers an all-female team of skilled con artists to pull off an ambitious heist at the Met Gala, aiming to steal a $150 million Cartier necklace.

It’s sleek, clever, and effortlessly cool, with a cast that brings charm and confidence to every scene.

If you love stylish heist films with a bit of humor and flair, this one is a must-watch.

The end!
